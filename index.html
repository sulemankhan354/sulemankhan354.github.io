About Me

I am a PhD candidate at Linköping University, Sweden, working in the area of cybersecurity for next-generation aviation communication systems. My research focuses on secure authentication, key agreement, secure handover, mobility management, and anomaly detection for critical air-ground communication infrastructures. My main work targets Controller–Pilot Data Link Communications (CPDLC), the L-band Digital Aeronautical Communications System (LDACS), and Future Communication Infrastructure (FCI), with an emphasis on practical security, formal verification, testing, and resilient aviation networking. In addition to my academic research, I have professional experience in digital forensics, network security engineering, security testing, IDS/IPS, risk and threat analysis, and incident response.
