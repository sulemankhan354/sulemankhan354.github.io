MONAD — Monitoring and Anomaly Detection of Data Streams and Decision Processes in Advanced Digital ATS

2024–2027 Swedish Transport Administration Cybersecurity for ATS

MONAD is a research project focused on improving cybersecurity in Air Traffic Services by addressing vulnerabilities in critical communication technologies such as ADS-B and ADS-C. The project applies advanced artificial intelligence techniques and real-world datasets to detect and classify anomalies in air-ground communications.

The work supports the proactive identification of security threats and strengthens the integrity, resilience, and trustworthiness of advanced digital ATS environments.

  • PartnersLFV, Automation Program II (Part C), and SEC-AIRSPACE (SESAR 3)

AiRMOUR — Sustainable Air Mobility and Emergency Medical Drone Operations

EU-Funded Project Urban Air Mobility Finalised

AiRMOUR is a European research and innovation project that explored safe, secure, quiet, and sustainable air mobility, with a particular focus on emergency medical services and real-life drone operations.

This project aligns with broader aviation digitalisation and future air mobility themes that are relevant to secure, resilient, and operationally acceptable next-generation airspace services.

  • ScopeResearch and validation of future air mobility concepts and solutions
  • RelevanceSupports understanding of security, operations, and ecosystem needs in emerging aviation environments
  • FundingEuropean Union Horizon 2020 Research and Innovation Programme
